A 5 foot tall, 1.5 kilogram rocket my classmate Emmett Hart and I built for our 2020 Intermediate Engineering class final project. The rocket was an EZ-I65 airframe equipped with a payload bay, a Raspberry Pi (a single board computer running Linux), a custom PCB I designed to hold our sensors and connect them to the Raspberry Pi's GPIO pins, and an IMU, temperature, and altitude sensor. The rocket flew on an H283 high power composite fuel rocket motor making 73 pounds of thrust, and the launch earned me my NAR Junior Level 1 high power rocketry certification.
